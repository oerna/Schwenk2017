What is Schwenking

It is more than just a way of doing BBQ. It is a way of life!

Schwenken is the Culture of a small country in the heart of Europe called Saarland. It is the very local way of doing BBQ. It is almost insulting to ask a Saarländer, if he "grills". A Saarländer would answer, NO, I "schwenk".

If you want to see it, check out this great video 🔗 to see how a Saarländer schwenks at home.
